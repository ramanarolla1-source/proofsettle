## [1.0.0] - Initial Release

### Added

- Introduced the ProofSettle Transparent Decision Intelligence architecture.
- Established the three-layer prediction and settlement model.
- Integrated Verified Sports Intelligence as the foundation for trusted operational decisions.
- Introduced the Decision Intelligence layer for deterministic rule evaluation and decision validation.
- Designed the Decision Journey workflow from verified sports intelligence through trustless on-chain settlement.
- Introduced Trust Receipts as transparent, user-verifiable records of operational decisions.
- Added cryptographic verification using Decision Artifacts and Merkle Tree structures.
- Defined the concept of a Permanent History of Transparency through Trust Receipt preservation.
- Published the initial product documentation, architecture, and technical specifications.
