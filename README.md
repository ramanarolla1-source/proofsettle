# ProofSettle

## Transparent Decision Intelligence for Prediction & Settlement

ProofSettle is a **Transparent Decision Intelligence Layer** that transforms **Verified Sports Intelligence** into **Trusted Operational Decisions** before **Trustless On-Chain Settlement**.

Rather than changing how prediction and settlement work, ProofSettle strengthens the decision journey between them—making every operational decision transparent, explainable, and independently verifiable.

## Why ProofSettle?

Every sporting event leads to countless operational decisions.

Prediction markets, fantasy sports, automated rewards, intelligent sports applications, and settlement systems all depend on trusted sports intelligence.

While users can usually verify the final outcome, they rarely have visibility into **how the operational decision was actually reached**.

ProofSettle was designed to solve this missing layer of transparency.

Instead of asking users to blindly trust operational decisions, ProofSettle enables anyone to inspect, understand, and verify the complete decision journey before settlement occurs.

---

# Product Philosophy

**Every Important Decision Deserves a Transparent Procedure.**

ProofSettle believes transparency should not begin after settlement—it should exist throughout the entire decision lifecycle.

# Trust Receipt

Every completed operational decision generates a **Trust Receipt**.

The Trust Receipt serves as a permanent transparency record, allowing users, operators, auditors, and regulators to understand exactly how a decision was produced.

A Trust Receipt records:

- Decision Identifier
- Match Information
- Verified Sports Intelligence References
- Rule Evaluation Summary
- Decision Artifact
- Cryptographic Verification
- Settlement Reference
- Verification Status
- Timestamp

Transparency should not end when settlement is complete.

ProofSettle preserves a permanent history of transparent decision-making through verifiable Trust Receipts.
