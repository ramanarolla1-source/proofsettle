# Technical Documentation

# ProofSettle

## Transparent Decision Intelligence for Prediction & Settlement

---

# 1. Introduction

ProofSettle is a **Transparent Decision Intelligence Layer** that strengthens prediction and settlement by transforming **Verified Sports Intelligence** into **Trusted Operational Decisions** before **Trustless On-Chain Settlement**.

Rather than replacing existing prediction or settlement infrastructure, ProofSettle introduces a transparent decision layer between them, making every operational decision understandable, explainable, and independently verifiable.

---

# 2. The Problem

Modern sports applications increasingly depend on automated operational decisions.

Prediction markets, fantasy sports, reward distribution, operator platforms, and intelligent sports applications all rely upon trusted sports intelligence.

Blockchain provides immutable settlement.

However, immutability alone does not explain **how the operational decision leading to settlement was produced**.

Users often see the final outcome but have little visibility into:

- What information was used.
- Which rules were evaluated.
- How the operational decision was validated.
- Why settlement was authorized.

ProofSettle addresses this transparency gap.

---

# 3. Solution Overview

ProofSettle introduces a dedicated Decision Intelligence layer that transforms verified sports intelligence into transparent operational decisions before settlement occurs.

Every decision follows a deterministic and verifiable workflow.

The final outcome is accompanied by a Trust Receipt that records the complete decision journey.

---

# 4. Three-Layer Architecture

ProofSettle intentionally separates responsibilities into three independent layers.

## Layer 1 — Verified Sports Intelligence

This layer provides trusted sports intelligence.

Current implementation utilizes **TxLINE**, including:

- Historical Sports Data
- Live SSE Match Streams
- Consensus Odds
- Standardized Machine-Readable Schemas
- Merkle Proof Verification
- validate_stat

This layer establishes the trusted foundation for operational decisions.

---

## Layer 2 — ProofSettle

ProofSettle operates as the Transparent Decision Intelligence layer.

Responsibilities include:

- Decision Intelligence
- Context Processing
- Smart Contract Rule Evaluation
- Business Rule Validation
- Decision Validation
- Decision Artifact Generation
- Cryptographic Merkle Tree Construction
- Settlement Authorization

This layer transforms verified information into trusted operational decisions.

---

## Layer 3 — Trustless Settlement

Settlement infrastructure executes approved operational decisions.

Current implementation targets **Solana**, providing:

- Smart Contract Execution
- Immutable Transactions
- Transaction Confirmation
- On-chain Settlement

ProofSettle does not replace settlement.

It strengthens the operational decisions that lead to settlement.

# 6. Decision Artifacts

Every completed decision generates a structured Decision Artifact.

The artifact contains:

- Decision metadata
- Rule evaluation summary
- Validation results
- Cryptographic references
- Settlement authorization

Decision Artifacts provide reproducibility before settlement occurs.

---

# 7. Cryptographic Verification

ProofSettle strengthens decision integrity through cryptographic verification.

Verification includes:

- Merkle Tree Construction
- Cryptographic Hashes
- Integrity Validation
- Tamper-Evident Decision Records

These mechanisms ensure operational decisions remain independently verifiable.

---

# 8. Trust Receipt

The Trust Receipt is the final transparency artifact generated by ProofSettle.

Rather than simply confirming settlement, it records the complete operational decision journey.

Each Trust Receipt preserves:

- Decision Identifier
- Match Information
- Verified Sports Intelligence references
- Rule Evaluation Summary
- Decision Artifact Reference
- Cryptographic Verification
- Settlement Reference
- Verification Metadata
- Timestamp

Trust Receipts enable users, operators, auditors, regulators, and future intelligent systems to inspect and verify how operational decisions were produced.

---

# 9. Core Design Principles

ProofSettle is guided by five engineering principles.

## Transparency

Operational decisions should be visible and understandable.

## Explainability

Every decision should clearly communicate how it was produced.

## Determinism

Identical verified inputs should always produce identical operational decisions.

## Verifiability

Operational decisions should be independently verifiable.

## Trust

Confidence should be earned through transparent procedures rather than blind acceptance.
